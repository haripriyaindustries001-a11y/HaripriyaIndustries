Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products and services

What is the durability of Cuddapah stone? ‚ñº
Cuddapah stone is highly durable and has been used for centuries in construction. It can last for generations with minimal maintenance. The stone has excellent compression strength and is resistant to weathering, making it ideal for both interior and exterior applications.
What finishes are available? ‚ñº
We offer multiple finish options including leather finish (textured and slip-resistant), polished finish (high-gloss), honed finish (semi-smooth), brushed finish, and natural finish. Custom finishes can also be created based on your specific requirements.
How are stones calibrated and what sizes are available? ‚ñº
Our calibration process ensures exact thickness and uniform dimensions for perfect installation. We offer standard sizes from 12"x12" to 24"x48", and can customize sizes according to your project specifications. All calibrated stones come with consistent thickness for seamless installation.
What is the minimum order quantity? ‚ñº
We accommodate orders of all sizes, from small residential projects to large commercial supplies. Minimum order quantities vary based on product type and finishes. For small orders, please contact our sales team for special pricing. Bulk orders receive attractive wholesale discounts.
How should Cuddapah stone be maintained? ‚ñº
Cuddapah stone requires minimal maintenance. Regular sweeping and occasional wet cleaning with mild detergent is sufficient. For polished finishes, avoid acidic cleaners and use pH-neutral stone cleaners. Sealing is recommended for interior applications to protect against stains.
What is your delivery timeline? ‚ñº
Standard delivery is 7-14 days depending on order quantity and location. Expedited orders can be processed within 3-5 days at an additional charge. International shipments take 15-30 days. We ensure safe packaging and timely delivery to your location.
Do you offer bulk discounts and wholesale pricing? ‚ñº
Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for bulk orders. The discount percentage varies based on order quantity and specifications. For large projects, we can provide volume-based pricing structures. Contact our sales team to discuss your bulk requirements and get customized pricing.
